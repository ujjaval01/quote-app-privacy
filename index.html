Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Quotes ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Daily Quotes does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or sensitive user data.

The app works completely offline and does not require user registration or account creation.

2. Local Data Storage

The app allows users to save favorite quotes. This information is stored locally on your device using local storage mechanisms and is never transmitted to any external server.

3. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notification Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Used to deliver daily motivational quote reminders.
  • Background Scheduling & Wake Permissions: Used internally by Android to schedule and display notifications at a specified time.

These permissions are used solely for app functionality. No personal data is collected, accessed, or shared through these permissions.

4. Internet & Network Usage

This application does not require an internet connection to function and does not transmit any user data over the internet.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or external databases.

6. Children's Privacy

Daily Quotes does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no storage of sensitive user information on external servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
